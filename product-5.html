Gallery / The whale and the sail

The whale and the sail

$250
Select size

A deep ocean blue — a whale rises from below while a white sail crosses the surface above. The composition grew from a single idea: two worlds that coexist without knowing each other.

Painted in layers, each one a different depth.

"A whale in the deep and a sail on the horizon — one travelling up, the other down. I've always been drawn to things that pass each other without touching."

— Dasha Orlinsky, artist
