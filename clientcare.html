Shipping

We ship worldwide from our studio in Canada, partnering with trusted carriers. Every order includes tracking information sent directly to you once dispatched.

Processing takes up to 2 business days. Exact delivery times are shown at checkout after entering your shipping address — so you always know what to expect before confirming.

Processing: up to 2 business days · Worldwide delivery

Packaging

Each artwork is packed the way it deserves — as a fragile and valuable object. Multiple protective layers, reinforced corners, and sturdy outer packaging ensure that what arrives in your hands is exactly what left ours.

Opening the parcel is part of the experience. We design the unboxing to feel like receiving something truly special — because that is what it is.

Protective layers · Reinforced corners · Luxury unboxing

Returns & Exchanges

If something does not feel right, please reach out to us first — we would rather find a solution together than have you feel uncertain about your purchase.

Returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery. As each piece is handmade and unique, conditions may vary by situation. Detailed terms are always handled in conversation, not through an automated process.

14-day window · Personal case-by-case care · Contact us first

Personal Support

This studio is run by a person, not a department. When you write to us, a real person reads your message, considers it, and responds with care. Not a template — a reply.

If your piece arrived damaged, if you have any question at all, or simply want to share your experience — we are genuinely here. Every message matters to us.

Real person · 24-hour response · Every message answered
